KCL Module Calculator

#JavaScript #CSS #jQuery

2015-08-23

Bored at the weekend, had come to the realisation in the week that KCL's weightings for different modules and years is too complex and that this website really needed some content. So I made this. Just a simple calculator, that asks the user for their grades - depending on how many years they've so far completed - and finds the average, and their highest and lowest modules (helpful for selecting modules for final year). Hopefully its useful to someone. Link
