I was bored at the weekend, and had come to the realisation in the previous week that my uni's (King's College London) weightings for different modules and years is too complex for their BSc Computer Science course (and that this website really needed some content). So I made this.


Just a simple calculator module calculator, that asks the user for their grades - depending on how many years they've so far completed - and finds the average, and their highest and lowest modules (helpful for selecting modules for final year). Hopefully its useful to someone.


Obviously, this is in no way sponsored, related or reflective of King's and the breakdown of the course is probably no worse or better than any other uni - but its still needlessly complex.
